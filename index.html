Contents of your German Intensive Course

In the German Intensive Course Online, you will learn and practice all the skills necessary for learning German as a foreign language. These include:

Speaking / Listening

Reading / Writing

Grammar / Vocabulary

Our teachers focus on interesting and varied lessons in which there is a lot of communication. With fun to success – this is the motto of our language school since 1999.

Excellent German teachers

Our German teachers are all native speakers who care about results. Every single student that chooses to work with us gets a personalized approach to get them to the next level in the German language. This ensures a confident use of the German language in class, perfect pronunciation and the necessary cultural background. Our German teachers are distinguished by their:

Personality

Experience

Education

Small class groups and interactive teaching methods guarantee that you will speak a lot and learn the German language effectively.

Levels intensive English courses

The levels of our intensive English summer courses in Amsterdam (or online) are based on the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) and are therefore internationally recognized. The intensive general summer courses cover a whole level and take 2 weeks. We offer intensive English summer courses from the beginners level up to the B2 level.

All the aspects of the language will be covered, so grammar, listening, speaking, writing and reading. However, we focus most on the speaking aspect.
